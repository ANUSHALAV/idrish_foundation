Project Free Library

IMPACT OF FREE LIBRARY

"Every year, we provide free books, both new and old, to 100 students. We organize book donation boxes and events to collect old books and ensure that there are comfortable sitting arrangements for needy students." We aim to establish Ambala's inaugural free library, providing both free books and a dedicated space for underprivileged students. Idrish Foundation, established by Neha Parveen on September 17th, 2017, in Ambala, had been quietly working towards its mission since 2015, even before adopting its name. Our primary objective is to provide education to underprivileged children.
