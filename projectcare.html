Project Care

project care

Project care is dedicated to nurturing the academic talents of children hailing from single-parent or orphaned backgrounds. We furnish them with vital educational resources, covering expenses such as school fees, clothing, and books. Additionally, we're committed to improving government schools. Annually, deserving youngsters will be granted an Educational Kit, and scholarships can be obtained through the Child Commission to support their educational journey.
