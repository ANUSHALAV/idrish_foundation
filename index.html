about us

IDRIS foundation a non for profit organization based in the UK, aims to raise funds and awareness for projects that are designed to improve the quality of life for refugees through the use of technology advances in Education and Health. The committee members include globally recognized philanthropists and prominent public figures. All current employees of IDRIS foundation are unpaid volunteers.

oure mission

The Idrish Foundation is an NGO devoted to advancing and facilitating education among children in poor and socially vulnerable communities. We strive to provide basic amenities and aid to children who cannot access them due to their lack of resources. Our primary objective is to provide quality education to every underprivileged child and to not let financial issues impact their knowledge gaining process.
